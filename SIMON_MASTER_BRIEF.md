## MY PRODUCTS — OVERVIEW

### 1. myhue.co *(Build first)*
**Energy-based app** measuring energy across four elements:
- **Spark** — ignition, enthusiasm, ideas
- **Glow** — warmth, connection, care
- **Root** — grounding, stability, process
- **Flow** — movement, adaptability, rhythm

**What makes it different:** Assessment via *conversation*, not traditional questionnaire. Ongoing dialogue helps users build skill across all four energies, not just their top preference.

**Target:** Individuals seeking self-awareness and energy optimisation.

---

### 2. LACE Assessment *(Build second)*
**Self-assessment tool** (also aggregates into team reports) measuring:
- **L** — Listening
- **A** — Accountability
- **C** — Collaboration
- **E** — Empathy

**Two dimensions measured:**
1. The extent to which LACE behaviours are deployed
2. The level of **decision debt** experienced in the team/organisation

**Target:** B2B and B2C. Individuals and teams. HR, L&D, leadership.

**Source framework:** The LACE Effect book by Simon Phillips (FRSA, FCIPD).

---

### 3. Change In 8 *(Build third)*
**8-minute process** for achieving goals while maintaining wellbeing.

**Target:** Individuals wanting structured, time-efficient personal change methodology.

2. **Revenue path must be clear** — every feature should have a visible line to income.
3. **Protect the IP** — frameworks, names, and methodologies belong to Simon Phillips / The Change Maker Group.
4. **Think scalability** — tools should work for 1 user and 10,000 users.
5. **Think ecosystem** — each product should reference and complement the others.
