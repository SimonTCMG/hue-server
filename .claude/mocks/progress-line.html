Anatomy and rules

  • Always all four colours. Both the track shimmer and the fill carry the full Spark → Glow → Tend → Flow palette at all widths. Nothing ever appears or disappears.
  • Subtle drift while idle. The colours flow gently from right to left across the line on a 14-second loop. Visible from the moment the screen opens — the empty track shimmers at low opacity (≈22%), the filled portion drifts at full saturation. Same animation, two intensities, in sync. Tells the user "this is alive, listening" without any explicit cue.
  • Drift is independent of completion. The line keeps moving while the user is mid-typing, mid-thinking, mid-anything. Coverage advance and colour drift are two separate animations on the same element.
  • Respects prefers-reduced-motion. Drift halts; the line still fills as coverage advances.
  • The line grows in width, not in colour count. What's changing as the conversation builds is how much of the strip is filled. The palette is a constant.
  • Same palette as the Results page card frames. Already established in the design system as "all four energies as one picture."
  • End labels. "STARTED" and "COMPLETE" anchor the line. Small letter-spaced stone caps. No number, no percentage, no tooltip.
  • Increment unit. 8 coverage points → 8 × 12.5% slices of fill width.
  • Completion. Line reaches "COMPLETE" + exchange floor reached → completion fires.
  • Quarterly retest. Line opens fully filled (ghost of last completion) at 30% opacity (drift continues), then dims to track and refills as the new conversation runs.
