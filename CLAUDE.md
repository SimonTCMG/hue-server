## WHAT WE'RE BUILDING

**myhue.co** — an energy-based app that helps people understand and develop their energy profile across four elements.

This is **Product #1** in Simon's portfolio. It must reach MRP and generate income before significant work begins on LACE Assessment or Change In 8.

---

## THE CORE CONCEPT

Most personality/energy tools use static questionnaires. Hue is different:

- **Assessment happens through conversation** — a natural, flowing dialogue, not a form
- **Four energy types are identified:** Spark, Glow, Root, Flow
- **The result is a starting point, not a label** — the ongoing conversation helps users build skill in all four energies, not just their dominant one
- **It's developmental, not diagnostic** — the app stays with the user after assessment

---

## THE FOUR ENERGIES

| Energy | Character | Represents |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| **Spark** | Ignition, enthusiasm, ideas | Starting, innovating, energising |
| **Glow** | Warmth, connection, care | Relating, nurturing, inspiring |
| **Root** | Grounding, stability, process | Structuring, delivering, sustaining |
| **Flow** | Movement, adaptability, rhythm | Adapting, progressing, navigating |

Every person has all four. The tool measures preference and deployment, then coaches development.

## PRODUCT PRINCIPLES

- **Conversational UX** — never feel like a test or a form
- **Warm and human** — the tone is curious, not clinical
- **Ongoing value** — the app must give reasons to return; assessment is the entry point, not the destination
- **Simple entry, deep value** — easy to start, rich over time
