Your conversations

What happens to what I say to MyHue?

Nothing leaves this conversation and reaches another human being. Not one person — not at MyHue, not anywhere — reads what you say. There is no moderation team. There is no inbox somewhere filling up with people's conversations. Nobody is watching.

Here is exactly what happens, step by step.

You type something. It travels to MyHue's server.

MyHue's server is where your profile lives — your energy picture, your conversation history. This is what allows MyHue to remember you between sessions. It is stored securely. No one at MyHue accesses individual conversations. Your employer cannot access it. Your manager cannot access it. If you use MyHue through work, all they ever see is a broad picture of team energy — never what you said, never what was asked, never what MyHue noticed about you. That is not a policy that someone could override. It is how the system is built.

Your words are then sent to the AI to generate a response.

The AI that powers MyHue's conversations is made by a company called Anthropic. This is the part people most often worry about — so here is what actually happens. MyHue never sends your name or email address to Anthropic. The conversation arrives there with no identity attached. Anthropic's system has no idea whose conversation it is. It processes your words, generates a response, and that is it. Anthropic does not store it beyond what is needed to run the service. They do not read it. They do not use it to train their AI.

So who actually sees what I say?

Nobody. The AI processes your words, generates a response, and Anthropic does not hold on to it. No human reads it. No human reviews it. What remains is your profile — held securely on MyHue's server, visible only to you.

One thing MyHue will never do.

MyHue is not a crisis service and does not pretend to be. If a conversation starts to feel heavier than an energy tool is the right place for, MyHue will say so — warmly, honestly — and point you toward somewhere better placed to help. That is the extent of it. Nothing gets reported. Nobody gets notified. The conversation remains yours.

The short version, if you want it.

No human reads your conversations. Ever. The AI processes them and Anthropic does not hold on to them. Your name is never attached to them. Your employer never sees them. MyHue is your Hue — and what you share here stays here.

Your profile

Your energy profile belongs to you — not MyHue, and not your employer if you use Hue through work. It travels with you if you change jobs. It stays yours if you stop subscribing. However you came to Hue, your profile is yours alone. You can request deletion of your profile and account data at any time by contacting hello@myhue.co.
