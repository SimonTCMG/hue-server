# Hue for Teams
*One-page client document · Updated April 2026*

---

## What it is

Hue is an AI-conducted energy assessment and ongoing companion. Through a natural conversation — not a questionnaire — Hue identifies how each person naturally tends to show up across four energy dimensions: Spark, Glow, Tend, and Flow.

The result isn't a label. It's a working model — a profile that helps people understand their own patterns and practise reaching for different energies in the moments that call for them.

---

## What your team gets

**Every individual receives:**
- A personal AI assessment (conversation, 10–15 minutes per energy)
- A full energy profile with personalised observations — not generic descriptions
- An ongoing AI companion, aware of their profile, available between sessions
- The ability to ask Hue anything about how their energy tends to show up in specific situations

**Their profile belongs to them.** It's not visible to their employer. It travels with them if they leave. This is a public commitment — not a policy that can be reversed.

---

## What you see as a team buyer

- **Aggregate energy picture** — how your team collectively tends to show up across Spark, Glow, Tend, and Flow
- **Composition analysis** — where the team reaches naturally, where deliberate attention helps, what your collective profile means for how you work together
- **Hue-generated team insights** — prompts and observations specific to your team's actual profile, not generic team development content

**You do not see individual results.** Ever. The team layer sits above individual profiles — you see the collective picture, not the personal one. This is what makes it an easier internal sell than any existing tool.

---

## How it's different

Most tools give you a static PDF and a day in a training room. Hue stays. The AI companion continues the conversation between sessions — asking about real situations, helping people practise flexing into less-preferred energies, building self-awareness through dialogue rather than theory.

No accreditation required. No annual re-certification. The assessment is conversational, the insights are specific, and the companion is always there.
